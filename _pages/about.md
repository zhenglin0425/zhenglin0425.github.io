**About me**

Welcome to Zheng Lin's homepage! I am now a postgraduate student at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Fudan University (FDU), Shanghai, China. I was awarded the Postgraduate National Scholarship in 2022. In August 2023, I will start my PhD studies at the University of Hong Kong (HKU).

**Research Interest**

My research interests include terahertz networks, vehicle-to-everything (V2X), edge learning(e.g., federated learning and split learning).
