About Me

Welcome to Zheng Lin's homepage! I am currently a first-year PhD student at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, the University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Xianhao Chen and Prof. Kaibin Huang. Before HKU, I was a master student of the Fudan INC lab led by Prof. Yue Gao and worked closely with Prof. Zhe Chen.
